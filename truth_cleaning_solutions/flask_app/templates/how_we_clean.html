How We Clean

The founder Jennifer comes from 22 years in the cleaning and quality indstry. A major goal for her and the team is to change the way house cleaning is done. We will bring a mix of hospital and hotel experience to ensure your property is as clean as possible.


One thing we pride ourselves on is ensuring excellent quality cleans. We're pretty sure you don't want to come back to a house that smells like a chemical bomb was just let of! Neither do we! All of our cleaning equipment is derived from natural ingredients such as coconut, aloe vera, and oranges. This gives you the peace of mind knowing that you can breath easily while you clean up and have no harmful chemicals to worry about.
